Training & Development

We have a training and development arm dedicated to personnel training.

Our aim is to improve your Human Capital Development through our tailored On-site, Off-site and Online training.

We create educational activities for clients to enhance the knowledge and skills of their employees while providing information and instruction on how to better perform their tasks.

Estate Management and Development

The H&B Properties Management and Development arm deals with Real Estate developments and offers benchmark property units. The core of our business is to develop and manage real estate projects. The property market in Nigeria is developing fast and, we are fully aware of the business potential.

We work with partners who have the proven expertise in developing retail, residential, leisure and, commercial building projects in major cities in Nigeria.

We are poised to exploit to the fullest the inherent potentials of the property market, by constantly raising our performance in the real estate industry.

Our goal is to remain the most professional and reliable Property Development Company in the country.

Logistics Management

Our logistics business model is designed to provide quality and cost efficient haulage and logistics services to our clients. This has been achieved by ensuring that every step of our clients logistics chain is managed and coordinated with adequate care, by making sure that our team of professionals see to it that goods in our custody are delivered in good condition and more importantly at the expected time.

Industrial Tailoring
