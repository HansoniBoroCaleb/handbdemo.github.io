About H&B Energy and Logistic Services Limited

H&B Energy and Logistic Services Limited was established in 2013 with the goal of becoming one of the best People and Business Management Consulting firms in Nigeria. With offices in Lagos and twelve other locations, we pride ourselves as specialists in our chosen field to be committed to the ideals of tapping new sources of value, which also means sharing ownership of outstanding results. We are driven by a sense of mutually beneficial relationships with our clients, with a view to creating high performance teams, and supporting business operations that conforms with industry best practice.
