Our team

We have a team of consumate professionals ready to work with you.

Mr Adeyemi Oshin ACIPM

MD/CEO

Yemi holds over 28 years of experience in Human Resource Management, Property & Estate Management, Outsourcing and Logistics, Vendor Management & Payments. Yemi started his Human Resources career with Flour Mills of Nigeria Plc between 1993 to 1999. He had a brief stint with Concord Press Nigeria Ltd as the National Sales/Circulation Manager before joining Intercontinental Bank Plc. (now Access Bank) where he was between 2000 -2013.

Yemi was the Pioneer Managing Director of Integrity Press Limited, a subsidiary of Intercontinental Bank. Yemi is a professional member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management (CIPM), Association of Outsourcing Practitioners of Nigeria (AOPN), Chartered Institute of Management (NIM); Academy for Entrepreneurial Studies (AES).

Yemi holds a B.Sc. degree in Psychology from the University of Ibadan. A Diploma in Business & Industrial Law from the University of Lagos and MBA in Human Resources Management from Lagos State University. He has also attended many Professional and leadership training programmes facilitated by institutions such as the Lagos Business School, London Management Centre (LMC) London and other chartered professional bodies both Local and International.

Adedeji Aderele ACIPM

Director

Deji’s Human Resources experience spans from Banking, Real Estate, Telecommunications, Consulting to Non-Governmental Organizations. He has expertise in manpower resourcing, training and organizational development. Deji at a time was the Regional Human Resource Manager for Intercontinental Bank Plc (Now Access Bank Plc) and Senior Manager Human Resources for Airtel CCO Nigeria, ISON BPO. With HR Specialization in Performance Management.

Deji holds a degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering and a Masters degree in Managerial Psychology from the University of Ibadan. He is an active member of the Society for Human Resources Management and an associate of Strategic Human Resources Managers in Nigeria. As a management consultant in human resources, he pioneered many projects both in the corporate and government establishment. He is a Principal Partner and Pioneer of Swift Employment Global Check, managing over 560 employees across 34 states in Nigeria.

Deji has also handled management retreats and trainings for conglomerates and multinationals in the course of his career. He is a seasoned HR practitioner, a strategist and motivational speaker. He currently oversees our North Central and FCT operations as Regional Director. He has attended several training programmes in diverse fields both locally and overseas.

Toyin Elebute

Director

Toyin holds a higher National Diploma in Business Administration from the Polytechnic Ibadan. He started his career at the Portland Paints and Products, a Subsidiary of the defunct West African Portland Cement (W.A.P.C.O) now Lafarge PLC (1990-1994).

In 1995, Toyin teamed up with his friends to form a public relations outfit known as O.M & T with their head Office in Surulere, Lagos. As the Client Service Director, he was the one interfacing between the Company and their Clients. Major clients were First Bank, First Trustees and First Registrars.

In 1999, Toyin moved to the United Kingdom where he had certificates in some Marketing/Supply courses. While working with Stanford Media in South-West London, he was in the Client Service Department of the P.R Company. His career development cuts across marketing and product development strategy. In 2003, he returned back to Lagos, Nigeria to establish his own Advertising Support/Public Relations Company named ZOTOK LIMITED till date.

Emeka Anagwu ACIPM

Chief Operations Officer

Emeka is a partner and head of our Business Development unit, as well as an SME Consultant. He is a strategic thinker and excels at process improvement of organizational systems. He is a stickler for excellence with a strong desire to replicate this by grooming others to achieve same. A client management expert who creatively sustains profitability for clients by providing exceptional services and experiences for them at all times. A strategic HR Professional with a strong bent of performance management and employee engagement.

A committed student of “Matt Bird’s” Relationology, which is a unique approach to achieving business growth through the power of customer and client relationships. He believes that relationships drive a world of possibilities. However, those relationships have to be nurtured with excellent and committed service.

Emeka is a consummate professional with 18 years experience in banking spanning Human Resources, Relationship Management and Branch Management. He has played both leadership and advisory roles which he brings to bear in developing customer systems for exceptional business performance. He is passionate about what he does and has the boldness to take on new ideas and try new things. Emeka has a BSC from the University of Nigeria and an MBA from ESUT Business School. He is also an Associate member of the Nigerian Institute of Management.

In the span of his career, Emeka has held various senior roles. This includes but is not limited to the following. He was at a time, Human Resources Manager SE & FCT for erstwhile Intercontinental Bank, HR Business Partner South Zone StanbicIBTC Bank, Regional Relationship Manager, Personal Banking South Zone StanbicIBTC Bank, as well as the Branch Manager Olu Obasanjo Branch, StanbicIBTC Bank with a superlative result in the period. In addition to being the Regional Director, South-South for H & B Energy and Logistics Services Limited, Emeka is currently a partner with Viral Business Consulting, bringing his experience to bear in providing solutions for clients across multiple areas of their businesses.
